How To Reallocate A Fixed Indexed Annuity

Request reallocation of a fixed annuity with this form.

Download Form

Instructions

Complete Form

Completed form must be returned within 30 days of the anniversary date.

Signatures

If the contract is joint-owned both owners must sign. If signing as Power of Attorney (POA) verification of the Power of Attorney signature (driver's license or Affidavit of POA) must be included. Signatures must be current-signed within 30 days of receipt at our service center.

Percentages

Please make sure to indicate whole percentage per option with the total equaling 100%

Submit form

Return the completed forms with any required documentation via U.S. mail, overnight mail or fax to Fidelity & Guaranty Life Insurance Company Service Center.

Confirmation

A confirmation of this reallocation will be mailed to the owners address of record upon completion.
