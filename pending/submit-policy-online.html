How To Submit A Policy Online

Yes, an application can be submitted online through our iPad app. Click here to download the FGL Pathfinder Life app or the FGL Pathfinder Annuity app. You can also pre-fill and download a bundled application from your browser on SalesLink. On SalesLink, click on sales tools, online application. Follow the steps to complete the application, then hit “download”, complete the application and submit to the company.
